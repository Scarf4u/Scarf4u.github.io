POS SERVICES

POS LAJU,J&T

For courier services for Pos, SCARF4U business choose Pos Laju courier and also J&T. Both of these couriers support the delivery of lids to our customers who pay through online transfer transactions or, payment at atm machines. We also give users the opportunity to make the choice of courier suitability they want. If you want to make a choice, the customer can leave a note in contact us for courier selection by including the recipient's delivery details.Thank You.

FAQ' POS SERVICES

How long does it take to deliver an item to the customer?

The time for the goods to reach the customer is approximately 3-5 days after the day the customer submits the order.

Are customers charged shipping charges if they want to choose a delivery courier?

No, customers can choose a courier by leaving a note in the contact us section.

Does the price given include shipping charges?

For customers who make payment by POS, We provide free shipping service to all customers who purchase with SCARF4U

If there is any damage to the parcel when the goods are received, what should the customer do?

Customers can take a photo of the item/ parcel from the courier involved along with customer details as proof and send it to Scarf4U email

How does the customer know the status of the goods?

Each item that has been processed, hq will send a tracking number in the customer's email. Customers can check the status of goods according to the type of courier provided/selected.
