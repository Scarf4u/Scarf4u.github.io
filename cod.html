CASH ON DELIVERY (COD) SERVICES

DHL

For Cash On Delivery (COD) courier services, SCARF4U businesses choose DHL courier. DHL is an American international logistics company providing courier, package delivery and express mail service, which is a division of the German logistics firm Deutsche Post.This courier supports the delivery of lids to our customers who make payment when the goods reach their hands.Thank You.

FAQ' POS SERVICES

How long does it take to deliver an item to the customer?

The time for the goods to reach the customer is approximately 3-5 days after the day the customer submits the order.

Are customers charged shipping charges if they want to choose a delivery courier?

For delivery via cash on delivery (COD), there is only one courier involved, namely DHL.

Does the delivery service have a shipping charges?

Yes, for customers who choose payment by cash on delivery (COD), the delivery charge is RM10. This is to avoid the risk of customers canceling last minute orders.

If there is any damage to the parcel when the goods are received, what should the customer do?

Customers can take a photo of the item/ parcel from the courier along with customer details as proof and send it to Scarf4U email

How does the customer know the status of the goods?

Each item that has been processed, hq will send a tracking number in the customer's email. Customers can check the status of goods according to the type of courier provided.
